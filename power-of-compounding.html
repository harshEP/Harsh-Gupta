Power Of Compounding

Power of compounding means earn interest on your invested money. So if you invest a certain amount in any financial instrument for example Mutual Funds which are very popular in India, and on invested money Earn Interest through the compounding effect, and you earn interest on your interest in the late year of your investment.

When you regularly invest over time, your returns could accumulate at a much faster pace.

Saving money and earning compound interest amount every year is good thing. But what if you were to invest a fixed amount every month? This small act could boost your returns over time. Let's find out how that is possible.

If not, then I will explain because, Harsh invested for longer period of time than Yash. That's the reason, Harsh generate more than 2 Crore with less amount but more time invested in market.
