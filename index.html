What is Share Market , Mutual Funds and Long Term Investing ?

Share Market

It is just same like any other market. In this market, we buy and sell Shares (ownership of companies) in very less price. It is the aggregation of buyers and sellers of shares, which represent ownership claims on Businesses.

Mutual Funds

It is an Asset Management Company that take money from all of us, retail investors and ordinary persons and invest our money in shares and generate good returns on our money. This corpus of funds is managed by an Fund Manager. The gains or loses on the investment are shared collectively by the investors in proportion to their contribution to their Fund.

Long Term Investing

Long Term Investing means invests our money for longer period of Time i.e. minimum 3 to 5 years and maximum there is no limit. In this, your money will compound by longer period of time and it will create huge corpus and generate wealth.
